The Event

What is Nisadya?

Nisadya is the annual national-level management fest organised by the Department of Management Studies, NIT Tiruchirappalli — a confluence of sharp minds, fierce competition, and transformative ideas from premier institutions across India.

From strategy simulations and finance challenges to marketing wars and consulting cases, Nisadya is where future leaders are tested. Each edition brings a fresh theme, new challenges, and an experience that outlasts the event.

A National Stage

Why Participate

Nisadya is more than a competition — it is a platform. Students from top institutions compete in events that mirror real business challenges, evaluated by industry professionals and academic experts.

The friendships forged under pressure, the lessons drawn from failure, and the clarity that comes from competing at a national level — these are the things that stay.

Annual Edition

Every Year, A New Vision

Each Nisadya is anchored to a central theme reflecting the most pressing challenges in the business world — shaping every event, case study, and conversation over two days.

This annual reinvention is what keeps Nisadya fresh, relevant, and consistently ahead of the curve.

Our Institution

The Legacy Behind Nisadya

National Institute of Technology

NIT Tiruchirappalli

Established in 1964, NIT Tiruchirappalli is consistently ranked among India's premier technical institutions. With world-class infrastructure, cutting-edge research, and a distinguished alumni network spanning every sector, NITT has shaped the careers of engineers, managers, and leaders for over six decades.

Department of Management Studies

DoMS, NIT Tiruchirappalli

The Department of Management Studies offers a rigorous MBA programme blending analytical depth with managerial acumen. Rooted in quantitative rigour and informed by industry practice, DoMS produces leaders equipped to navigate complex, ambiguous business environments.

The department's clubs — Vittiya and others — foster a culture of competition and continuous learning that finds its highest expression in Nisadya.
